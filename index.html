Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to charter a private jet?+

Private jet charter costs vary based on aircraft type, distance, and availability. Light jets start from $3,000/hr, midsize jets from $5,000/hr, and heavy jets from $9,000/hr. Contact JetLux for a precise quote based on your specific itinerary.

How far in advance do I need to book?+

JetLux can arrange a charter with as little as 4 hours notice. For peak seasons and popular routes, we recommend 48–72 hours to ensure ideal aircraft availability and catering preparation.

What are empty leg flights?+

Empty legs are repositioning flights — aircraft traveling to their next booking with no passengers. JetLux lists these at up to 75% off standard charter rates. Sign up for alerts to access deals as they appear.

Which destinations does JetLux serve?+

JetLux operates globally, covering over 40,000 airports worldwide. Popular routes span North America, Europe, the Middle East, and the Caribbean — but if there's an airstrip, we can get you there.

Is private jet charter safe?+

Absolutely. JetLux exclusively works with FAA and EASA-certified operators. All aircraft undergo rigorous maintenance schedules, and our pilots hold ATP certificates with thousands of flight hours.
