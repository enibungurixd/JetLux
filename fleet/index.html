Common Questions

Fleet FAQ

What size private jet do I need?+

Light jets suit trips under 3 hours with up to 8 passengers. Midsize jets handle transcontinental routes with added cabin comfort. Heavy jets offer full stand-up cabins for 10–16 passengers on long-haul routes. Ultra-long-range jets fly any destination nonstop with hotel-like interiors.

Can I inspect the aircraft before my flight?+

Yes. JetLux can arrange a pre-flight aircraft walkthrough at your departure FBO. We also provide full specs, photos, and crew bios before you confirm your booking.

How many passengers can a private jet carry?+

Capacity ranges from 6 passengers on a light jet to 19+ on an ultra-long-range BBJ. Exact seating depends on the specific configuration of each aircraft — our team will match you to the right fit.
