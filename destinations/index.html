Destinations FAQ

Your Questions Answered

Can I fly a private jet to any destination?+

JetLux has access to over 40,000 airports worldwide, including many private and regional airfields not served by commercial airlines. If there is a runway, JetLux can typically arrange a private jet charter to that destination.

What are the most popular private jet routes?+

Our most requested routes include New York to Miami, London to Dubai, Los Angeles to Las Vegas, Paris to Monaco, Miami to the Bahamas, and Geneva to St. Moritz. Corporate corridors like New York to Chicago and London to Paris are also frequent requests.

Do I need a visa to fly private internationally?+

Visa requirements are the same as for commercial travellers and depend on your nationality and destination. JetLux's concierge team advises on entry requirements and can help coordinate ground-side immigration procedures at private FBO terminals.

Which airports does JetLux use?+

JetLux primarily uses private FBO terminals and executive airports to bypass commercial congestion — Teterboro near New York, Farnborough near London, Le Bourget near Paris, and Al Maktoum in Dubai, among thousands of others worldwide.
