Booking FAQ

How quickly will JetLux respond to my quote request?+

JetLux typically responds within 15 minutes during business hours and within 1 hour overnight. For urgent same-day flights, call our 24/7 hotline directly for the fastest response.

Is there any obligation when I request a quote?+

None at all. Requesting a quote is completely free and carries zero obligation to book. We provide aircraft options and pricing — you decide if and when to proceed.

What information do I need to get a charter quote?+

We need your departure and destination cities or airports, travel dates, passenger count, and any special requirements. The more detail you provide, the more tailored your quote will be.
